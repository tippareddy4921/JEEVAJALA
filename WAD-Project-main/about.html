ABOUT US

Save Water Save India is an initiative started in 2023 by Shreyansh and Tippareddy from BMS College of Engineering, Bangalore, Karnataka. Main motive to start this initiative is saving water resource for the future generation of India. We collect complaints for any kind of water wastage at state level and try our best to raise the issue and pressurize the government to take some action on it!! Soon we will be starting collecting local water wastage complaints from metro cities and in 2026, we are targetting to collect complaints for water wastage from pan India. We also show ways to conserve water at an individual level.
